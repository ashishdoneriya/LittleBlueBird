What is LittleBlueBird ?

It's your wish list online. It's the only place you and your family need to go to find out what everyone wants.

How Does It Work?

Create events in LittleBlueBird like Christmas, your birthday, your anniversary... (you get the idea).

Add your friends and family to these events so you can see their lists, and they can see yours.

How Do I Start?

Just go to www.LittleBlueBird.com and sign up.
 
Once you create your account, we recommend creating an account for everyone in your family. (Yes, you can create accounts for other people - just one of the things that sets LittleBlueBird apart from every other wish list and gift registry site out there.)

The LittleBlueBird Difference

Events

Most sites have no concept of an event - you just create a list of stuff you want. But that's not how wish lists work, is it?

You don't just want stuff, you want stuff for Christmas, for your birthday for your anniversary. No other site has the concept of an Event, like Christmas or your birthday, which is a little strange if you ask us. After all, you're asking for something because it's Christmas or because it's your birthday.

And what happens when the event is over? LittleBlueBird will still display the event. Only now, it will show you what you got for that event!

Specify Multiple Recipients

Can you specify that a gift has multiple recipients? With LittleBlueBird you can. With any other site? Don't count on it.

But isn't it realistic that a husband and wife would want something together, or that grandparents would give a gift for all the grandchildren to share? Of course.

Create Accounts for Other People

With LittleBlueBird, you can create accounts for other people. This comes in handy when you have families with small children that can't create their own accounts.

Finding Someone's Wish List

How do you see someone else's list? On LittleBlueBird, all of your friends and family are listed under each event you are participating in. Just click someone's name. On any other site, you'll have to enter each person's name or email address or user handle.

LittleBlueBird Won't Spoil the Surprise!

What good is a wish list site that tells you what you're getting? LittleBlueBird will never spoil the surprise. You will never know what you're getting until you've gotten it. On any other site ...sorry. There's a pretty good chance you'll know exactly what you're getting from Mom, from Dad, ...from everyone. Other sites don't even try to maintain the surprise
