LittleBlueBird Privacy Policy

Q: What personal information do we require?

A: We only require you to enter your name, gender, and email address.

Q: Why do we need to know your name?

A: If you're that paranoid, enter a fake name.

Q: Why do we need to know your gender?

A: So we can display a gender-appropriate silhouette for people who don't provide profile pics. Also, so we don't have to litter our site with his/her's, he/she's and Mr/Ms's ;)

Q: Why do I have to enter my email address?

A: So you can recover your password if you forget it. LittleBlueBird will also send you email notifications about gift-giving events that you are participating in.

Q: Can I turn these notification off completely?

A: Yes, from the "My Account" page, see the "Email Preferences" section.

Q: Do I have to authenticate my email address?

A: No

Q: Who does LittleBlueBird share my personal information with?

A: No one

Q: Really?

A: Really

Questions? Shoot us an email at info@littlebluebird.com
