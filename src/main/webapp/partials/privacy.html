LittleBlueBird Privacy Policy

What personal information do we require?
We only require you to enter your name, gender, and email address.
Why do we need to know your name?
If you're that paranoid, enter a fake name.
Why do we need to know your gender?
So we can display a gender-appropriate silhouette for people who don't provide profile pics. Also, so we don't have to litter our site with his/her's, he/she's and Mr/Ms's ;)
Why do I have to enter my email address?
So you can recover your password if you forget it. LittleBlueBird will also send you email notifications about gift-giving events that you are participating in.
Can I turn these notification off completely?
Yes, from the "My Account" page, see the "Email Preferences" section.
Do I have to authenticate my email address?
No
Who does LittleBlueBird share my personal information with?
No one
